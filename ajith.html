Ajith

Ajith Kumar, commonly known as Ajith, is a prominent actor in the Indian film industry, particularly in Tamil cinema. Born on May 1, 1971, in Hyderabad, India, Ajith has established himself as one of the leading actors in the South Indian film industry. Ajith began his acting career in the late 1980s with small roles in Tamil films. He gained attention with his performances in supporting roles and gradually transitioned to lead roles. He has appeared in a wide range of films across various genres, including action, drama, romance, and thriller.
